# VIT Bhopal — Examination Rules & Attendance

## Minimum Attendance Requirement
Students must maintain at least 75% attendance in every individual subject to be eligible to sit for that subject's Final Assessment Test (FAT). VTOP tracks attendance daily and flags students approaching the danger zone. If attendance in a subject drops below 75%, the portal automatically debars the student from writing the FAT for that subject.

## Attendance Relaxation for High CGPA
Students with a CGPA of 9.0 or higher are eligible for an attendance relaxation, allowing some flexibility without being penalized in the same way as other students. This relaxation does not remove the requirement entirely -- students should confirm current terms with the Academic Office.

## Evaluation Structure
Academic evaluation at VIT Bhopal happens continuously through the semester rather than through a single final exam: CAT-1 and CAT-2 (Continuous Assessment Tests, similar to mid-terms), digital quizzes and assignments, and the FAT (Final Assessment Test) held at the end of the semester.

## Debarred Status
A student debarred from writing the FAT due to insufficient attendance is awarded an "N" grade for that course and must re-register for the course in a later semester to clear it.

## Revaluation / Answer Script Review
Students who wish to have their answer script reviewed after results are declared can apply through the university's examination process, which involves an online application and a prescribed fee per subject. Exact steps and the fee amount are announced by the Controller of Examinations each semester -- check the current circular on VTOP.

## Course Registration
Attendance percentage is calculated from the date of course registration, so timely registration on VTOP directly affects how much attendance buffer a student has that semester.
