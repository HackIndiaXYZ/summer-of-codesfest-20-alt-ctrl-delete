# VIT Bhopal — Certificates, Transcripts & General Requests

## Academic Transcripts
Transcripts are requested through VTOP: log in, go to Services → Transcript Request, fill in your requirements, and generate the invoice. Then go to Online Payments → Payments to pay for the request. Once payment is confirmed, the Controller of Examinations office processes the transcript. Students who want a soft copy by email should select the "Scan Copy" option when submitting the request.

## Alumni Transcript Requests
Alumni (students who have graduated) can also request transcripts through VTOP using Alumni Login with their Registration Number, Alumni Email ID, and Date of Birth. If the alumni email is not known, it can be updated by writing to the examinations helpdesk.

## Bonafide Certificate
Bonafide certificate requests are made through VTOP under the Services section, similar to transcript requests. The request is processed by the examinations / academic office once submitted.

## Migration Certificate / Medium of Instruction Certificate
These are issued after final results are declared and all dues are cleared. Requests are typically routed through the university's Academic Records section.

## VTOP Portal
VTOP ("VIT on TOP") is the single platform for academics, attendance, fee payments, hostel services, and most administrative requests. Most certificate, transcript, and payment requests at VIT Bhopal go through VTOP rather than in-person paperwork.

## ID Card / Other Requests
For requests not available directly on VTOP (like ID card reissue), students should approach the Academic Office directly, since the exact process can vary by request type.
