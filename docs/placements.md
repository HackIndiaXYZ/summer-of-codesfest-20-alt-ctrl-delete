# VIT Bhopal — Placements

## Overview
VIT Bhopal's Placement and Training (PAT) Office, working with the central VIT Career Development Centre, coordinates campus placements for final-year and pre-final-year students. Placement preparation -- aptitude, communication, and technical training -- typically begins from the third semester onward.

## Placement Highlights
Since 2019, VIT Bhopal has had over 4,300 students placed, with more than 2,500 students receiving Super Dream or Dream offers (these figures include both internship and full-time offers). The highest package achieved by a VIT Bhopal student is Rs. 70 LPA. Microsoft has recruited from VIT Bhopal for 6 consecutive years, and the university has had international placements for 4 consecutive years.

## Recent Placement Statistics
In the 2024-25 placement season, VIT Bhopal recorded over 1,100 offers, including 400+ Super Dream offers (packages above Rs. 10 LPA), with a placement rate above 90% among eligible, registered students. The average package was approximately Rs. 8.5 LPA.

## Top Recruiters
Recruiters that have visited VIT Bhopal include Microsoft, Amazon, Intel, Apple, IBM, Cisco, Goldman Sachs, Bosch, Accenture, Deloitte, Cognizant, and TCS, among others.

## Placement Categories
VIT Bhopal classifies offers into tiers -- regular offers, Dream offers, and Super Dream offers (typically packages above Rs. 10 LPA) -- with dedicated preparation tracks for each tier through the PAT Office.

## Internships
Internship placements are also coordinated through the PAT Office and are considered alongside full-time offers in the university's overall placement statistics.

## Contact
For placement-specific queries, questions can be directed to the PAT Office. Company-wise or year-wise historical statistics beyond what's listed here should be confirmed directly with the PAT Office, since exact figures are updated each placement season.
