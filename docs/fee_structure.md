# VIT Bhopal — Fee Structure & Payment FAQ

## B.Tech Tuition Fee by Branch Group
VIT Bhopal groups its B.Tech branches into two fee groups. Group A includes Bioengineering, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering, Production and Industrial Engineering, Fashion Technology, and B.Des. (Industrial Design) -- tuition is Rs. 1,73,000 per annum, plus a one-time refundable caution deposit of Rs. 3,000, making the first-year total Rs. 1,76,000.

Group B includes Aerospace Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering (and its Automotive/Mechatronics specialisations), Electronics and Communication Engineering (and its specialisations), and Computer Science and Engineering along with all of its specialisations (AI & ML, Cyber Security, Data Science, Robotics, IoT, Blockchain, Business Systems, Bioinformatics, and others) -- tuition is Rs. 1,95,000 per annum, plus the same Rs. 3,000 caution deposit, making the first-year total Rs. 1,98,000.

## VITEEE Rank-Based Fee Categories
Within these groups, the exact tuition a student pays can also depend on their VITEEE rank category (Category 1 through 5), with better ranks qualifying for lower fee categories and larger scholarship waivers. Category 1 (top ranks) pays the base group tuition; higher categories pay progressively more, up to roughly Rs. 4,93,000 per annum for Category 5 in some branches. Students should confirm their exact category and fee on VTOP after counselling.

## No Fee Hike Policy
Once a student's fee category is fixed at admission, that same tuition amount is charged every single year of the programme -- 1st year, 2nd year, 3rd year, and 4th year all cost the same. There is no year-on-year fee increase.

## Fee for Each Year of the Programme
Because of the no-hike policy above, your yearly fee is simply your group's annual tuition, every year: Group A branches pay Rs. 1,73,000 per year (1st through 4th year alike). Group B branches (including all Computer Science and Engineering specialisations, Aerospace, IT, Mechanical, and Electronics and Communication) pay Rs. 1,95,000 per year (1st through 4th year alike). The Rs. 3,000 caution deposit is only charged once, in the first year. Students on a higher VITEEE fee category (2 through 5) pay their assigned category amount instead, and that amount is likewise the same every year.

## Scholarships
VIT Bhopal offers merit-based tuition fee waivers ranging from 25% to 100%, linked to VITEEE rank and Class 12 board performance. The STAR Scholarship scheme offers a 100% tuition fee waiver to state and district toppers from government schools. Students with a VITEEE rank in the top 10 are typically eligible for a full 100% tuition waiver (caution deposit and other charges still apply).

## Payment Method
Fees are paid online through the VTOP student portal: Online Payments -> Payments / Fee Receipts. Accepted modes include net banking, UPI, and debit/credit card.

## Fee Due Dates
Fee due dates are announced each semester through official circulars and are also visible on VTOP under the payments section. During admission/counselling phases, balance fee deadlines are announced per counselling round. Students should check VTOP directly for the current due date.

## Education Loans
VIT Bhopal has an on-campus Indian Bank presence to help students access education loans for tuition and hostel fees.
