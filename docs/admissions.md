# VIT Bhopal — Admissions (VITEEE)

## Entrance Exam
Admission to B.Tech programmes at VIT Bhopal is through VITEEE (VIT Engineering Entrance Examination), a national-level computer-based test conducted by VIT for its Vellore, Chennai, AP, and Bhopal campuses. Around 2.5 lakh candidates appear for VITEEE each year. The exam is 2 hours 30 minutes long with 125 multiple-choice questions: Mathematics/Biology (40), Physics (35), Chemistry (35), Aptitude (10), and English (5). The current marking scheme awards +4 for a correct answer and -1 for a wrong answer.

## Eligibility
Candidates need a minimum of 60% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (or Biology) in Class 12 or equivalent, with at least 50% specifically in Mathematics or Biology. There is no caste-based reservation in VITEEE admissions -- selection is purely rank-based. Candidates must have been born on or after 1 July of the relevant cutoff year (check the current year's notification for the exact date).

## Application Process
Applications are submitted online at viteee.vit.ac.in. The application fee is approximately Rs. 1,350, payable online (netbanking, card, or UPI). Applicants upload a photograph and signature, choose their preferred exam city, and complete a slot booking (OTBS) to download their admit card closer to the exam.

## Counselling Process
After results are declared, candidates participate in online counselling conducted in multiple rank-based phases (typically five phases). During counselling, candidates choose their preferred campus, programme, and fee category based on their rank and seat availability. VIT Bhopal and VIT-AP generally have more relaxed closing ranks than Vellore and Chennai, so students with higher ranks still have a realistic chance at these two campuses. The counselling registration fee is approximately Rs. 5,900 (non-refundable), separate from the tuition fee.

## Seat Confirmation
After seat allotment, candidates pay an advance/balance portion of the fee to confirm their seat within the stipulated deadline. Once a seat is confirmed and fee paid, branch, category, or campus cannot be changed. Original documents (VITEEE admit card, rank card, Class 10 certificate for date of birth proof, Class 12 marksheet, and any applicable certificates) must be produced at document verification, either at campus reporting or when requested by the Admissions Office.

## Score Validity
VITEEE scores are valid only for that year's admission cycle and cannot be carried forward to the next year -- a candidate who does not secure admission must reappear for the following year's VITEEE.
